technical background

More information regarding Josh's academic and software projects can be accessed via the computer science page on this website linked above.

personal background
Beyond his major, Josh has also found expression through music, architecture, and poetry. All artistic endeavors can be accessed via the navigation bar. When he was young, Josh learned to play the piano. During college, he decided to learn guitar from scratch. He has since been producing and mixing music on his own time.

He is also a second degree Black Belt in TaeKwonDo, a Korean martial arts.
During middle and high school, he ran track, specializing in sprints and jumping. He went to state for the 4x400 relay. Having lived most of his life in Oregon, he is often inspired by the natural beauty around him. And this can be found in his creative work. With so many trails near him, he loves to go out and hike, mountain bike, packpack, kayak, paddleboard, and snowboard with his close friends and family.
