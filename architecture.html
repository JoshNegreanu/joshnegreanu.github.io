purpose
dreamdesign was the result of a month-long senior project that encouraged students to learn something new. In this case, Josh decided to learn how to design a house and CAD it in Revit.
project info
Along the way, he consulted three local architects and discussed his ideas, incorporating their feedback into the final design. He also experimented with Enscape, a rendering engine, to provide realistic depictions of the house. The structure can also be explored in virtual reality.
With this newfound interest in architecture, Josh is once again exploring the idea of designing a new house. More info soon.
